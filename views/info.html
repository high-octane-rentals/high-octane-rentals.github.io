Our History

High Octane Rentals was started by our family of snowmobile enthusiasts who often invited friends to join us for trips to the mountains or lakes. Our friends often told us that they couldn't rent the kind of quality equipment they enjoyed with us.

We answered this need by opening a business that provides high-end snowmobile and Ski Doo equipment for rent at a reasonable cost to those who love the sport but can't justify buying their own equipment. High Octane Rentals continues to expand. We have a rental fleet that will meet the needs and wants of customers of all skill levels for both winter and summer enjoyment!

It's our goal to allow everyone to have great outdoor experiences by providing quality equipment, fair pricing, and excellent service.
